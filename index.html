Learn More About Donations
Shopping online? The Amazon Smile program is an easy way to support Santa Clara Valley Blind Center, at no cost to you! It works like this - you visit www.smile.amazon.com, create or log in to your Amazon account, and choose Santa Clara Valley Blind Center as your preferred charitable org. When you make a purchase through smile.amazon.com, .5% comes back to us via quarterly electronic funds transfer. It's the same Amazon online shopping portal and shopping experience!
