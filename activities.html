Activities

SCVBC provides a wide spectrum of recreational, social, and therapeutic activities that encourage socialization, independence, and increased quality of life for our clients. Activities include exercise, outings, luncheons, sewing, ceramics, Yoga and Wing Chun Kung Fu and more!

We have many activities at our Center. Wednesday is Client Day at the Center where our clients enjoy camaraderie, make new friends, become mentors, and socialize. Speakers are scheduled who provide information on available community services, public announcements, introduce new technology and discuss advocacy issues.

For more information about our activities please click the links on this page or call us at 408-295-4016 x204.

Outings And Luncheons

SCVBC has many outings, luncheons, and events. Each month, at least one field trip to an interesting site, often outside of the county, is scheduled. On occasion we arrange for an overnight outing.

There are many opportunities to join us for lunch. Restaurant outings are arranged for the second Thursday of each month. On the fourth Thursday you can join clients for a social lunch at the Center. In addition, the Center provides a summer picnic, and numerous special "theme" lunches around holidays or special events.

Free meals are provided on Wednesday through the County Senior Nutrition program to our senior clients (60 or older) and their spouses. Non-seniors may pre-order a meal for $4.50 Meals must be ordered by 5:00 pm Monday.

For more information about our activities please click the links on this page or call us at 408-295-4016 x204.

Exercise

SCVBC attempts to match individuals with the kind of exercise that will benefit them the most. Whether you prefer yoga or stretching, or walking indoors or out, we’ve got what you need!

Our exercies and activities include:

  • Yoga
  • Low-impact stretching
  • Wing Chun Kung Fu
  • Walking

For a schedule of weekly exercise courses and activities, click on the Calendar link on the main navigation bar contact us at 408-295-4016 x204.

Ouilting and Sewing

SSCVBC has a class that meets at the Center each week to work on their own quilting, sewing and needlepoint projects. Qualified volunteers are here to assist when necessary. Enrollment is open year round. For more information, contact us at 408-295-4016 x204.
