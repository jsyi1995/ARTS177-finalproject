Rehabilitation Services

Braille Instruction

SCVBC provides Braille instruction for individuals needing to learn Braille to read, as well as for individuals who wish to learn Braille to transcribe. For over 40 years, SCVBC has worked closely with the Braille Transcription Project, Brailling materials and teaching Braille. Classes are taught weekly at the Center. For more information call 408-295-4016 or write to info@visionbeyondsight.org

Councelling

SCVBC provides a wide variety of counseling and support for individuals losing their vision. Individualized counseling, support groups, and sharing groups are offered weekly to match the needs of individuals facing the traumatic and often devastating onset of vision loss or blindness.Individuals who feel particularly desperate or depressed should seek assistance immediately from their physician or local health crisis agency. For more information about SCVBC’s series of support and counseling, call 408-295-4016 or contact info@visionbeyondsight.org

In Home Support

SCVBC is proud of its legacy of providing quality, responsive, and responsible volunteer support for individuals in their homes. SCVBC will match an in-home volunteer to a client for one-on-one assistance reading mail, filing paperwork, and much more. Volunteer support is always needed and appreciated both at the agency and in individuals’ homes. To learn more about volunteering at SCVBC, click on the “Volunteering” link on the main navigation bar.

Living Skills

SCVBC offers a panorama of rehabilitative skills development to make living easier and safer for individuals losing their vision. Some of the types of adaptive training include:

  • Cooking
  • Sewing
  • Money Management
  • Transportation
  • Labeling
  • Hygiene And Dressing

For detailed information on these services, or to obtain a schedule of classes, call 408-295-4016 or write to info@visionbeyondsight.org

Orientation And Mobility

SCVBC has fully certified Orientation and Mobility instructors. Often, the instruction is free of charge or SCVBC can set up an affordable plan on a sliding scale. Instruction occurs where the individual needs it, and hours are scheduled to accommodate the individual. Whether you are a client of the Department of Rehabilitation or simply need to find out how to get to a new store, we have the answer to your mobility needs! For more information call for an intake at 408-295-4016 or write to info@visionbeyondsight.orgAlso, check out our line of mobility and other adaptive devices in our store! Call 408-295-4016.

Technology And Computer Instruction

There are constant advancements made in the technology focused world of today. Some of these advances require specialized adapted technology for blind or visually impaired users to access them. Increasingly, more cost-effective technologies become accessible that make performing daily tasks easier. From telephone, email and closed-circuit television (CCTV) to high-tech computer and electronic note-taking instruction, SCVBC can help you with your technology training and assessment needs! For more information call 408-295-4016 or write to info@visionbeyondsight.org
