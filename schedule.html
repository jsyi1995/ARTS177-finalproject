Services and Training Classes

Adapted Living Skills Training

Classes (cooking, basic sewing, in-home by appt. only) are available.

Accessible Technology Training

Computer classes and low-technology solutions. $30/Qtr.

Counseling

Individual and family counseling are available.

Orientation and Mobility Training

One-on-one training: proper cane use and guide techniques to increase mobility in the home and in the community.

Braille Instruction

Individualized instruction on reading braille.

Information and Referral

Resource information on helpful products and services

In-Home Assistance

Volunteers are available to help clients in their home, with reading and paperwork.

Long-term Access Equipment Loan Program

Check out CCTV’S as available, $20 per month. Call Dawn at 408.295.4016 x202 for more info.

Peer Support Groups

Facilitated support groups for independence and daily living.

Resources

Resource materials, product catalogs, on-site Store with inventory.
