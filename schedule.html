SCVBC is a community-based organization dedicated to increasing the confidence, independence, and quality of life of the blind and visually impaired. SCVBC tackles complex vision loss issues comprehensively through rehabilitative, educational, and recreational services. Our services, activities, and classes are available to all visually impaired adults living in Santa Clara County. One-on-one assistance is available on a limited basis. Core rehabilitative services are provided at no cost to the client. Recreational/therapeutic activities are free or at a minimal cost.

Services and Training Classes


  • ADAPTED LIVING SKILLS TRAINING Classes (cooking, basic sewing, in-home by appt. only) are available.
  • ACCESSIBLE TECHNOLOGY TRAINING Computer classes and low-technology solutions $30/Qtr.
  • COUNSELING Individual and family counseling are available.
  • ORIENTATION AND MOBILITY TRAINING One-on-one training: proper cane use and guide techniques to increase mobility in the home and in the community.
  • BRAILLE INSTRUCTION Individualized instruction on reading braille.
  • INFORMATION AND REFERRAL Resource information on helpful products and services
